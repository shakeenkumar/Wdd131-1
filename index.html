Overview of Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry is a city located in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Known for its scenic views of the Godavari River, it's a cultural and historic hub. The city is famous for its temples, cuisine, and bustling markets.

General Information

  • Country: India
  • State: Andhra Pradesh
  • Coordinates: 17.0027° N, 81.7701° E
  • Area: 16.5 km²
  • Population: 500,000 (Approx.)
  • Time Zone: IST (Indian Standard Time, UTC +5:30)

Interesting Facts

  • Rajahmundry is known as the cultural capital of Andhra Pradesh.
  • It is named after Raja Raja Narendra, a Chalukya dynasty king.
  • The Godavari River flows through Rajahmundry, adding to its historic significance.

Top Attractions in Rajahmundry

  • Godavari Bridge: The second longest road-cum-rail bridge in India.
  • Markandeya Temple: A significant ancient Hindu temple.
  • Papi Hills: Known for natural beauty and scenic views along the Godavari River.
  • Kadiyali Beach: A peaceful beach destination.
