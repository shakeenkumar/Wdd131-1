About Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry, also known as Rajamahendravaram, is a historic city in Andhra Pradesh located along the Godavari River. Known as the "Cultural Capital of Andhra Pradesh," it is famous for its rich heritage, beautiful landscapes, and serene river views. Key attractions include the iconic Godavari Bridge, ISKCON temple, and the enchanting Pushkaralu festival.
